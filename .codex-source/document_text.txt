[Normal] ABOUT WINDMILLS
[Normal] The Philippine Camping Ground
Close Enough. Far Enough.
[Normal] Windmills Camp Grounds is the go-to camping destination for the modern Filipino adventurer — close enough to Metro Manila, yet worlds away the moment you arrive. Perfect for barkada getaways, family reunion weekends, and corporate office outings. We provide the grounds, the scenery, and the facilities. You bring the tent, the crew, and the good vibes. Two locations, two experiences — both unforgettable.
[Normal] Our Two Locations
[Normal] Tanay, Rizal — The Highland Camp
[Normal] Set in the cool emerald highlands of Tanay, Rizal — dramatic mountain terrain, misty mornings, cascading waterfalls, and sweeping Sierra Madre views. Just 2 hours from Manila.
[List Bullet] By Car: Via Marcos Highway (Marikina–Infanta Hwy) — approx. 1.5–2 hours depending on traffic.
[List Bullet] Public Transport: Bus to Cubao, then jeepney or UV Express to Tanay — approx. 2–2.5 hrs from Cubao.
[List Bullet] Address: Brgy. Cayabu, Tanay, Rizal 1980, Philippines
[List Bullet] Contact: +63 910 987 5807  ·  tanay@windmillscamp.ph
[Normal] Amadeo, Cavite — The Coffee Country Camp
[Normal] Perched in the rolling coffee-farm hills of Amadeo — the Philippines' barako capital. Crisp highland air, Taal views, and easy access from CAVITEX.
[List Bullet] By Car: Via CAVITEX or SLEX–Sta. Rosa exit — approx. 1.5 hours from Makati.
[List Bullet] Public Transport: Bus to Tagaytay, then jeepney or van for hire toward Amadeo — approx. 20 mins from Tagaytay.
[List Bullet] Address: Brgy. Dagatan, Amadeo, Cavite 4119, Philippines
[List Bullet] Contact: +63 910 987 5807  ·  amadeo@windmillscamp.ph

[Normal] ON-SITE FACILITIES
[Normal] We've Got the Grounds Covered
[Normal] Both camps provide clean, well-maintained facilities so you can focus on what matters — enjoying the outdoors. You bring your gear; we'll take care of the rest.
[Normal] 🚿 Clean Comfort Rooms
[Normal] Well-maintained CR and shower facilities with hot water — available to all campers throughout your stay.
[Normal] 🔥 Firepits & Firewood
[Normal] Every campsite comes with a dedicated firepit. Firewood is provided — just bring your lighter and camp recipes.
[Normal] 🚗 Parking Area
[Normal] Secured, on-site parking available for all registered campers. Each site comes with at least one parking slot.
[Normal] 💡 Ground Lighting
[Normal] Ambient pathway lighting throughout the grounds for safe navigation after dark without ruining your night sky view.
[Normal] 🛒 Camp Store
[Normal] Forgot something? Our on-site store carries basic camping supplies, snacks, instant noodles, drinks, and firewood top-ups.
[Normal] ☕ Coffee & Snack Bar
[Normal] Fresh brewed barako and local snacks available every morning. Amadeo camp serves beans straight from the farm.
[Normal] 🗑️ Waste Management
[Normal] Segregated waste bins across all grounds. We follow Leave No Trace principles — campers are expected to do the same.
[Normal] 🏥 First Aid Station
[Normal] Basic first aid kit available at the camp office. Emergency contact numbers posted at all entry points.
